Welcome

I am the face behind the camera,

I am so happy you have found me, your future photographer. My name is Jacquelyn and I have been an avid photographer since I was seven years old.

One of my favorite parts of being a photographer is meeting families and becoming a part of their moment. I have taken photos of high school seniors who are now married that invited me to capture their wedding moments.

Take a look at my gallery to see if my style of imagery is something that you would be proud to hang on your wall. I am already looking forward to hearing from you. We have memories to capture.
